Juno App Privacy Policy

At Juno, your privacy is our priority. We are committed to not using any tracking mechanisms, nor do we collect, monitor, sell, lease, or distribute any personal information about our users to third parties. Your interactions with the app remain confidential.

While using Juno, which facilitates access to YouTube content, your browsing experience is also subject to the privacy policy of YouTube.

Should you have any inquiries or need further clarification, please don't hesitate to reach out to us directly.
